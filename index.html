01 — About

From the lab bench to the model.

I'm a Chemical Engineering master's student at the University of Pennsylvania, finishing in May 2026. My research sits in bio-inspired materials — most recently developing polydopamine-coated hemp fibers as a sustainable approach to functional coatings.

Before Penn, I earned my Chemical Engineering degree at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College in Mumbai and spent time as a research engineer at the Bhabha Atomic Research Centre, working on thermal processing and process control. I like problems that need both hands-on experimentation and a model to make sense of the data.

Focus areas
